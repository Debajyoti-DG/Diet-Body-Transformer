Be Flexy

We advise you to stick to the following :

Strength Training

  • 5-10 minutes of dynamic stretches

  • 20 - 30 minutes Strength Exercises : Squats, push-ups, bent-over, planks (Each of them should be repeated so that there are 3 sets of 10 - 12 reps for each exercise)

  • 30 - 45 minutes Cardio : Running, Cycling or swimming. This should be 1-2 minutes of high-intensity cardio followed by 10 minutes of low- intensity ones. Do them for 45 minutes in cycles.

  • 10-15 minutes Active recovery : Engage in light activities like walking, yoga, stretching to initiate the healing process

  • Rest : You need adequate rest between every set of exercise you perform. This is preferrably 5 - 10 minutes based on your body's needs.

    Stay Hydrated

    Water is The Elixir of Life. Imagine your body as a well-oiled machine. Just like any other machine, it needs a constant supply of fuel to function optimally. In the case of your body, this "fuel" is water. Water makes up approximately 70% of your body weight and is involved in nearly every bodily function. From regulating body temperature to aiding in digestion, water is the elixir of life. Drink 10 glasses of water throughout the day.
    • Enhances Physical performance
    • Mental Clarity and Focus
    • Radiant Skin
    • Weight management
    • Prevent health issues

    Get Adequate Sleep

    Get 8 hours of sleep daily. Sleep is not a luxury, it is a fundamental pillar of health. The importance of adequate sleep cannot be overstated. It's a holistic remedy for both body and mind, contributing to physical vitality, emotional equilibrium, and cognitive acuity. So, let us all embrace the sanctity of sleep and cultivate the habit of a restful night's slumber, for in those quiet hours, we unlock the boundless potential of our well-being.
    • Physical restoration

    • Enhanced Immunity
    • Improved physical performance
